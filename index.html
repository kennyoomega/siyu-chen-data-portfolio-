What I built

What I'm building now
ML Data Quality & Governance Framework
In Development
A YAML-driven framework that sits before any model training and acts as a quality gate. Every run is automatic, auditable, and produces a structured JSON artifact — so there's always a clear record of what data was used, what checks passed or failed, and whether the pipeline is safe to continue. Hard gates block the pipeline on critical failures; soft gates warn without stopping.
